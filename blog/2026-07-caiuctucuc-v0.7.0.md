# CAIUCTUCUC v0.7.0: Knowing Is Not Proving

*Draft for greenshoegarage.com, with release v0.7.0*

This is the release the whole design was aimed at, the one where the mystery stops being a thing you solve and becomes a thing you have to prove.

The back half opens with a night in the records room. If you warned Pelham Rood, because Fenwick showed you a procurement list with a living man's name on it and you did the decent thing, then the shape with the razor finds the schoolmaster awake and armed with the constable's dog-headed cane, and the rest of the game has a scared, cooperative, living witness in it. If you did not, the morning finds him dead with the re-inked plat page in his hand, and at Rank 2 the records room goes cold and gives up two words in a voice still surprised about it: HE MEASURES. Either way the county narrows to two men, and only one of them measures for a living.

Then Crane's torchlit revival at the fort, and mid-sermon the ground groans where every soul present can hear it, and deniability is over, and the courthouse is open for the laying of a case.

The trial is arithmetic, and I am proud of how plainly the game shows its work. Physical evidence weighs double: the mismatched plat, Feig's testimony about town nails on a gentleman's last, the rubbings Fenwick sells you from the coffin Coombs should not have opened, or, my favorite, five brass benchmarks hunted across the county that re-derive the true line with no witness at all, because mathematics does not perjure. Conviction wants six. Pure Rank 0 legwork gets you exactly six, which is the design's oldest promise kept: the skeptic can hang him. A thinner file hangs the jury instead, and then Cresap appears at your elbow with the elders' offer already drafted, and you can let them bury it, and the act closes on a peace with poison in it, and the game does not pretend otherwise.

Witnesses lift a thin file too, and every one is earned: Beall testifies sober and believed if you spent the coffee and the patience on him, the chainman's widow swears to the letter if you handed her brother's effects over complete when she could not have checked, and Judge Kent tips the bench if you found his sealed record and chose absolution or leverage, two buttons that do the same arithmetic and very different things to the man.

Provoke Gantt instead, once your file is heavy enough for him to notice, and receive the politest threat in the game, and then survive the Narrows overlook at dusk, and Beall stops needing convincing, because nobody ambushes a stranger over mathematics.

The hue and cry finally grew its whole ladder: level three musters the militia with drums off the square, level four posts a bill with your coat on it and a professional reading it, and Shanks still poles the flat for a penny to the bank where Maryland law gets seasick.

Eighty five tests. One of them failed during development because I wrote an assertion that contradicted itself about whether three should hang a jury. The verdict function knew the law better than I did.
